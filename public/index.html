SC 05 About

I run the whole shoot.

I’ve spent 15 years behind the camera, and I’ve got the skills and the kit to match. Hire me and you get me: I plan the shoot, run the camera, and cut the final piece. One point of contact from the first call to final delivery.

I shoot on professional cinema gear, including the Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 6K, so every frame has that cinematic look. I’ve worked every role on a set: director, producer, director of photography, camera operator, gaffer, grip, sound operator, editor. So I can take your project from pre-production all the way to final export, or slot into a crew you already have.
