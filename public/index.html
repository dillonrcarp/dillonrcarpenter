About

A working filmmaker, not an agency.

I believe in the power of video to tell compelling stories and capture important moments. With 15 years behind the camera, I’ve built the skills — and the kit — to bring your brand or event to life without agency overhead.

I shoot on professional cinema equipment, including the Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 6K, so every frame has that cinematic look. And because I’ve worked every production role — director, producer, director of photography, camera operator, gaffer, grip, sound operator, editor — I can carry your project from pre-production planning through the final export, or slot into the crew you already have.
